Consultant, Strategy & Technology — Konrad

A next-generation digital consultancy role blending strategy, UX, and technology. Collaborate across Strategy, Project Management, UX Design, and Technology to guide clients through the Design Thinking process and deliver human-centred, cutting-edge user experiences.

About the role

You thrive at understanding complex problems, brainstorming with cross-functional teams, shaping impactful end-user experiences, and supporting technical delivery and implementation.

What you’ll do

  • Support projects from rapid-to-market initiatives to large digital transformations.
  • Help lead/participate in design thinking workshops with clients.
  • Translate business challenges and goals into clear UX/technology solutions.
  • Research processes, success factors, and competitive landscape via interviews and desk research.
  • Contribute to project management, coordination, and polished documentation/presentations.
  • Act as a design/technology thought partner on emerging trends.

Qualifications

  • Minimum 2+ years relevant experience in strategy, design, and/or technology engagements.
  • Demonstrable passion for user experience, digital design, and technology.
  • Strong client collaboration and communication (written and verbal); comfortable presenting.
  • Analytical mindset, strong problem-solving, and self-motivation.
  • Ability to work both collaboratively and independently; appreciation for great digital design.

UX Designer — G Adventures

Hands-on role focused on traveller journey from inspiration to booking. Turn insights into flows, wireframes, and prototypes in a cross-functional Marketing & E-commerce team.

About G Adventures

The world’s largest small-group adventure travel company (30+ years), with a mission to change lives through travel and benefit local communities.

What you’ll do

  • Design intuitive, user-friendly experiences that support the traveller journey.
  • Create user flows, wireframes, and prototypes; run usability reviews and benchmarking.
  • Ensure accessibility, SEO best practices, and performance considerations.
  • Collaborate with PMs, Designers, Developers, and Content; present rationale to stakeholders.
  • Maintain reusable assets in Figma and balance quick updates with larger redesigns.
  • Stay current on UX/UI trends and advocate for user-centered design.

Desired skills

  • 3+ years in UX/product design (e-commerce or content-rich sites ideal).
  • Proficient in Figma; strong UX principles and accessibility.
  • Experience translating insights into customer-centered solutions; strong communication.
