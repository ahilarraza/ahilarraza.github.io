A next-generation digital consultancy role blending strategy, UX, and technology. Collaborate across Strategy, Project Management, UX Design, and Technology to guide clients through the Design Thinking process and deliver human-centred, cutting-edge user experiences.

About the role

You thrive at understanding complex problems, brainstorming with cross-functional teams, shaping impactful end-user experiences, and supporting technical delivery and implementation.

What you’ll do

  • Support projects from rapid-to-market initiatives to large digital transformations.
  • Help lead/participate in design thinking workshops with clients.
  • Translate business challenges and goals into clear UX/technology solutions.
  • Research processes, success factors, and competitive landscape via interviews and desk research.

  • Demonstrable passion for user experience, digital design, and technology.
  • Strong client collaboration and communication (written and verbal); comfortable presenting.
  • Analytical mindset, strong problem-solving, and self-motivation.
  • Ability to work both collaboratively and independently; appreciation for great digital design.
